Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 May 2026

1. What data we collect

We collect your name, email address, phone number, delivery address, and order history when you create an account or place an order on MechChef.

2. How we use your data

Your data is used to process orders, send delivery updates, and improve our platform. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Payments

All payments are processed securely by Stripe. MechChef does not store your card details.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to keep you logged in and to understand how our site is used. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. Your rights (UK GDPR)

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. Email us at privacy@mechchef.co.uk

Terms & Conditions

Last updated: 25 May 2026

1. About MechChef

MechChef is a marketplace connecting customers with home cooks in Harrow and Newham, London. We are not a restaurant. Food is prepared by independent home cooks.

2. Ordering

Orders must be placed at least 2 hours before the chosen delivery slot. Once confirmed, orders cannot be cancelled unless the cook is unable to fulfil them.

3. Payments & Refunds

All payments are taken at time of order. Refunds are issued within 3–5 business days for cancelled orders. MechChef takes a 15% platform fee from each order.

4. Food Safety

All MechChef cooks must hold a valid Food Hygiene Certificate (Level 2 minimum) and comply with UK Food Safety regulations. MechChef verifies this before approval.

5. Allergens

Allergen information is provided by cooks. Customers with severe allergies should contact the cook directly before ordering. MechChef cannot guarantee allergen-free preparation environments.

6. Governing Law

These terms are governed by the laws of England and Wales.
